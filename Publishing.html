Masterclass Aim

To equip aspiring writers with basic knowledge of the narrative nonfiction genre (with a focus on biography, autobiography and memoir) for application in their individual book writing and publishing endeavours.

Masterclass Content Outline

  • Reasons and benefits of book writing
  • Introduction to narrative nonfiction
  • Biography, Autobiography and Memoir
  • Planning and Research
  • Timelines, Outlines & Drafts
  • Language, Style & Techniques
  • The Editing Process
  • Intellectual Property & Copyright issues
  • International Standard Book Number
  • Front Matter & Back Matter
  • Endorsements, Foreword & Preface
  • Design & Layout
  • Publishing Options & Cost Implications
  • Economics of Book Publishing
  • Print versus Online Publishing
  • Book Marketing and Distribution

Facilitator

Samuel Mayinoti is a Windhoek-based writer and publisher with 18 years of relevant experience. An author of 10 books, six of which within the biographical genre, he has also been involved in several book projects as project manager, developmental editor, ghost writer and publisher. With practical knowledge of the book publishing value chain, Mr. mayinoti has in the past six years conducted in-person workshops for aspiring authors in Namibia. He serves on the Literature Committee of the National Arts Council of Namibia.
